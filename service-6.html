Moving Services (Movers)

Professional Relocation Services (Movers)

Green Bins Randago Enterprises offers trusted and efficient movers and relocation services for businesses and institutions across Kenya. Whether you're moving a small office, a large corporate setup, or specialized equipment, we are fully equipped to handle your move with precision, care, and professionalism.

Our Commercial Moving Services Include:

  1. Office Relocation: – We help businesses move to new locations smoothly, ensuring minimal disruption to operations. From furniture to IT equipment, we pack and transport everything securely.
  2. Retail and Shop Relocation: – Whether you run a shop, showroom, or outlet, our movers handle the transportation of merchandise, display units, and inventory with care.
  3. Corporate and Institutional Moves: – We assist schools, NGOs, hospitals, and government offices with organized and scheduled relocation plans tailored to their needs.
  4. Packing and Unpacking: – Our team provides high-quality packing materials and professional packing services to ensure your items are protected during transit.
  5. Loading and Offloading: – We have trained personnel and the right tools to safely load and offload heavy or fragile items.
  6. Furniture Assembly and Arrangement: – Once on site, we help reassemble and position furniture and equipment according to your specifications.

Whether you’re relocating across town or to another county, Green Bins Randago Enterprises is your trusted partner for stress-free and secure commercial moving services.
