About Us

6+ Years of Experience

Green Bins Randago Enterprises is a dynamic and environmentally-conscious service company that specializes in a comprehensive range of essential services aimed at promoting cleanliness, hygiene, and convenience for both residential and commercial clients. Our core services include:

  1. Garbage Collection – We offer reliable, scheduled waste management and disposal services tailored to meet the needs of households, estates, and businesses. Our goal is to support a cleaner and greener environment through efficient and eco-friendly waste handling solutions.
  2. Fumigation Services – We provide expert pest control and fumigation solutions using safe and effective methods. Our services target a wide range of pests including cockroaches, termites, rodents, bedbugs, and mosquitoes to ensure healthy living and working spaces.
  3. Sanitary Pad Collection – We are committed to supporting menstrual hygiene management by providing discreet, timely, and hygienic sanitary waste collection services to institutions, offices, and schools. Our services comply with health and environmental safety standards.
  4. Cleaning Services – Our professional cleaning teams handle a variety of cleaning needs including residential cleaning, office cleaning, post-construction cleaning, and carpet and upholstery cleaning. We use high-quality, eco-friendly cleaning products and modern equipment to deliver sparkling results.
  5. Movers Services – Green Bins Randago Enterprises also offers local and regional moving services. Whether it’s home relocation, office transfers, or delivery of large items, we ensure safe, secure, and timely transportation of goods with minimal disruption.

We have comprehensive experience and a team of skilled environmental professionals dedicated to delivering high-quality services. Through our network of recognized disposal facilities, we provide efficient, cost-effective, and environmentally sound solutions in garbage collection, fumigation, sanitary pad collection, and cleaning services.
