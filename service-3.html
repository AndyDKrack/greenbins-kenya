Cleaning Services

Professional Cleaning Services

At Green Bins Randago Enterprises, we offer comprehensive cleaning services tailored for both residential and commercial spaces. Whether it’s a private home, office, or commercial building, our skilled team ensures your environment is spotless, fresh, and hygienic.

Our cleaning services include:

  1. Carpet Cleaning – Deep cleaning to remove stains, dust, and allergens, restoring the freshness and appearance of your carpets.
  2. Terrazzo and Floor Cleaning – Intensive scrubbing, polishing, and sanitizing of terrazzo and all floor types for a sparkling finish.
  3. Upholstery and Sofa Set Cleaning – Gentle yet effective cleaning of all types of seats, including fabric and leather sofa sets, office chairs, and more.
  4. Wall Cleaning – Removal of stains, dust, and dirt buildup to maintain clean and presentable walls.

We use safe, high-quality cleaning products and modern equipment to ensure the best results. Whether you need routine cleaning or a one-time deep clean, we've got you covered.
